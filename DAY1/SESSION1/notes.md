#Tracking of the problem 
- Vehicle problem

If Vehicles=300 and wheels=500(which includes both 2W and 4W).
Find the number of 2w vehicle and 4w vehicle.

Given:

V = TW + FW

W = 2TW + 4FW

TW = V - FW

Substitute in W equation:

W = 2(V - FW) + 4FW

W = 2V - 2FW + 4FW

W = 2V + 2FW

2FW = W - 2V

FW = (W - 2V)/2

Substitute V = 300, W = 500

FW = (500 - 2(300))/2

FW = (500 - 600)/2

FW = -100/2

FW = -50

TW = V - FW

TW = 300 - (-50)

TW = 350
